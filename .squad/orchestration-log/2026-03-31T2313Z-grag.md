## Work Summary

Implemented two high-impact persist bottleneck mitigations on top of DEC-027 (prepared statement caching + SQLITE_STATIC):

### R3: Batch Drain
- **Change:** Modified `cmd_index.cpp` drain loop to dequeue ALL available results from completion queue under a single lock, rather than one-at-a-time
- **Rationale:** Reduces lock/condition_variable overhead and enables better batch processing
- **Files:** `src/cli/cmd_index.cpp`
- **Impact:** Measurable reduction in lock contention overhead

### R4: Cold Index Skip DELETE
- **Change:** Added `Persister::enable_cold_index_if_empty()` to detect fresh/cold DB; sets `cold_index_` flag to skip DELETE step in `persist_file()` for empty tables
- **Rationale:** DELETE on empty tables incurs B-tree lookup and FK cascade overhead per file despite being a no-op
- **Files:** `src/index/persister.h`, `src/cli/cmd_index.cpp`
- **Impact:** Direct per-file persist cost reduction
